SAKURA ENTERTAINMENT — TELEGRAM BOT SETUP

1. In Telegram, open @BotFather and send /newbot.
2. Copy the bot username and token.
3. Add these environment variables in Vercel:

NEXT_PUBLIC_TELEGRAM_BOT_USERNAME=YourBotUsername
TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN=YourBotFatherToken
TELEGRAM_WEBHOOK_SECRET=CreateAtLeast16LettersNumbers_Underscore-Hyphen
NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_URL=https://sakura.a3group.sg

4. Deploy the website.
5. Put the same values in .env.local on your computer.
6. Run: npm run telegram:setup
7. Open your bot, tap Start, and send /chatid.
8. Add the returned number in Vercel:

TELEGRAM_ADMIN_CHAT_ID=YourChatID

9. Redeploy.

The bot supports:
/start
/book
/promotions
/hours
/contact
/chatid

Website table bookings are delivered to TELEGRAM_ADMIN_CHAT_ID.
Never share TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN publicly.
